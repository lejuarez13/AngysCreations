What We Do

Angy's Creations is a small business dedicated to the art of handcrafting exquisite home and holiday decorations. With a focus on handmade design, they create a range of unique pieces, from intricately designed wall hangings to intricately detailed holiday ornaments. Each item is lovingly handcrafted by Angy, ensuring that every piece exudes a distinct charm and character.

Home Decor

Each creation is handmade with love, showcasing the versatility and beauty of paper as a medium for interior design. Some home decor pieces made in the past range from delicate paper flower arrangements to 3D wall frames. Each creation is completely customizable, from the color of the paper used to the types of flowers created. Names and phrases can also be added truly making each item unique!
